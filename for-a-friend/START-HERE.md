# Your wedding website — start here

Everything you need is in one place, and it's not this file:

## 👉 **<https://github.com/eugenionerelli/wedding-invite-starter-kit>**

That's a complete, working wedding invite site — cover, your story, a
countdown, the day's schedule with Google Maps buttons, a photo gallery,
and an RSVP form — that you make your own and publish for **free,
forever**.

---

## What you'll actually do

**You are not going to learn web development.** The approach is
deliberate:

1. **An AI assistant does all the technical work** — Claude Code or
   Codex, about $20/month, one month is enough. It installs what's
   needed, sets the site up, publishes it online, and handles anything
   technical you hit later. There's a copy-paste first message that takes
   you from nothing to a live web address.

2. **You do the design in a visual editor** — click, type, drag. It runs
   on your own computer, it's free and open source, and it edits your
   real site directly. No account, no subscription, nobody else holding
   your work.

The two guides, in order, are the first two links in that repo:
**AI-SETUP.md**, then **HOW-TO-USE-THE-EDITOR.md**. The second one also
covers *how far you can push your assistant* — with concrete examples,
because most people ask for far less than they could get.

---

## Getting your own copy

On that page, click the green **"Use this template"** button (not
"Fork"). That gives you a clean, independent copy with your name on it.
Then follow AI-SETUP.md.

That's genuinely it. 🥂
